My Story

My programming journey started early. In high school, while others were just playing games, I was figuring out how to build them—creating DIY logic games, calculators, and custom music players. That curiosity evolved into a deep dive into computer science.

Today, I am a Software Engineer who thrives on versatility. Whether it's crafting memory-safe, blazing-fast microservices in Rust, building scalable enterprise systems in Java and .NET Core, or designing reactive frontends with ReactJS, I love the entire stack.

I don't just write code; I engineer solutions. From parsing data pipelines to handling complex state management, I focus on building software that is secure, efficient, and user-centric.
